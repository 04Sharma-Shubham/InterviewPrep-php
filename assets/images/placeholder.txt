## Image Sources
You can find suitable images from:
- Unsplash (unsplash.com)
- Pexels (pexels.com)
- Pixabay (pixabay.com)

Make sure to use images that are:
- High quality and properly sized
- Free to use (check licensing)
- Relevant to independent cinema
- Consistent in style and tone
